PROJECT TEAM
Redeveloping a pedigree brand through the power of web development and holistic marketing

Pimlico Plumbers approached Blue Frontier looking to design and develop a new website that would reflect the start of a new age at Pimlico Plumbers and incorporate the aesthetic of their parent company, Neighbourly UK.

With their growing range of home improvement and maintenance services catering for both the commercial and domestic markets in central and suburban London, Pimlico Plumbers was looking for a digital agency that could provide ongoing holistic marketing to help boost their reach and reputation.

Other key objectives for the rebirth of Pimlico Plumbers' brand included increasing brand collateral by way of professional Graphic Design and Digital Marketing services to support them in their goal to reach new customers and generate more qualified leads.
